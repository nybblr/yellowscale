Equipment

Great art is easy to spoil with poor tools, so I shoot on professional quality equipment to ensure your event is captured in the highest quality possible.

For the geeks, I shoot on the Canon 5D Mark III with the 70–200mm L F/4 and 24–105mm F/4 IS L (Canon’s top glass) lenses, 430EX II flash, softbox and reflector.
